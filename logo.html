Mining Mindfulness

A health coach and nutritionist asked me to create this logo as she is currently building her online business and wanted a logo that incorporates health, wellbeing and mindfulness. I chose Yellow and Teal for the colour palette. We associate yellow with sunshine and it represents light. It creates a feeling of hope, happiness and wisdom. Yellow evokes an optimistic sense of well being and natural light. It is airy, radiant and atmospheric. Teal is commonly used to represent open communication and clarity of thought.The blend of colors combines the calmness of blue together with the balance of green, making it a unique shade that is revitalizing and rejunevating. On closer inspection you can see the mandala faintly in the backround and the roundness and shading is like a reset button.

Michelle More

Michelle More is an inspirational speaker and life coach. Her logo has both intricate and simple details. The archway of the doors symbolise moving through difficulties or challenges, entering into new spaces and opportunities. Doors and openings provide the transition point to change. The outline of the door that appears in orange shows an M. The third eye above the doorways symbolises the gateway to higher consciousness and the state of enlightenment. The color teal is commonly used to represent open communication and clarity of thought. Orange is associated with youth, vitality, friendliness, and being open-minded.

Ghost Town

The video is shot with a fish eye lense which gave me the idea to make it round. I took a more literal approach to the design, making it look like an old western backdrop, under the eary moonlight.
