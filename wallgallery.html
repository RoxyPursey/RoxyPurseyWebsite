Client 1

A client had a beautiful family photoshoot and wanted to display these images on 2 seperate walls leading up their staircase.They wanted a simple geometric design using only black, white and pewter frames. I measured up the walls and built the design in Sketch-Up. Once they approved the shape of the design, I added the assorted frames so they had a visualisation of what the wall gallery display would look like before going ahead with the production. Doing visual mock-ups helps manage client expectation, gives a realistic perspective and is easy to make revisions before production.

Client 2

This client also had a beautiful photoshoot and wanted a wall gallery display that was assymetric with a sense of flow. They wanted a design created that could be used on both walls. The client provided a brief and photographs of the space so I could create a colour palette. I went to the clients house and took wall measurements and designed the space in Sketch-Up.
