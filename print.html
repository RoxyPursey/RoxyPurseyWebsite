Nutrisense Pamphlet

Nutrisense is a health tech company thats offers programs with expert guidance to help you interpret your own body's data.
Their product is a continuous glucose monitor (CGM) that monitors your glucose 24/7.
Nutrisense needed an informative pamphlet that goes into their product box. The pamphlet has clear instructions, is easy to read and elimated customer service questions related to the product.
