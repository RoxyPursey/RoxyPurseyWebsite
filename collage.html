Scar

One of my favourite designs I created was for a New York-based client who loved photographing wildlife. He traveled to Africa and captured some of the most beautiful imagery I had ever seen. I created a 'Peter Beard' inspired collage called 'Scar'.
I fell inlove with the story about the old rugged lion called 'Scar'. I felt like he deserved to be front and centre of the piece. Scar's mane was damaged from all the wars he fought against other male lions and I wanted to 'beautify' it. I decorated his mane with finger prints in the earthy colours. The black and white border surrounding Scar is inspired by the Ndebele tribe who are known for their strong geometric designs.
I gathered different textures, animals skins, Images, polaroids, and excerpts of his travel diary and layered these, creating a beautifully vibrant, truly African montage.

Chile

The same New-York based client loved his 'Scar' piece so much that he asked me to create a similar piece of his trip to Chile. Layering his spectacular imagery of Patagonia's, succulents, llamas and the Milky way. The black and white border is Aztec inspired and coupled with the imagery celebrated this amazing South American Adventure.

60th Birthday Tribute

When it comes to gifts for special occasions, it's hard to find something that is both meaningful and personal. Sometimes the usual materialistic things don't quite cut it. I created this special piece for a client's father who was celebrating 60 magical years on this earth. This is a collage that features photographs(old and new), the symbolism of favourite music, books, movies, travels, quotes and anything and everything that embodies this individual. It's a visual masterpiece of the greatest hits of your life and it will be treasured for years to come.
