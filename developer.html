Couch Flight - Hackathon

Built using React.js and API
This app was created in a 24hr hackathon I did at WCS.
Since we've all been locked up at home and unable to travel. We decided to create an app that would allow you to travel from your couch. Couch Flight was born and with the power of the internet and webcams set up all over the world you can visit these destinations from the comfort of your own home.
Couch Flight offers a generous selection of webcam access to all your favourite places or even places you didnt know existed. You can search by Country or Category or just pick a Random Cam and watch the world go by...
