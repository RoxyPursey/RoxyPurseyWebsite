Safari Album - Maasai Mara

To visit the Maasai Mara is to experience the original spirit and essence of ‘safari'. The Mara is a timelessly unspoiled wilderness with endlessly beautiful views, boundless game animals, and colourful birdlife. There is something about being completely immersed in the bush that makes you profoundly grateful and exhilarated to be alive. With every new sunrise over the grassy savannah and forested hills, the potential for incredible moments to unfold and memories to be created is refreshed, as each day is so often dramatically different from the next. The Mara teaches you to celebrate the joy of living. This client wanted a coffee table book that represented his incredible trip to the Mara. I layered photographs and textures to create this African adventure that you can page through with awe and wonder.

Safari Album - Boys Trip To Africa

This father and his sons embarked on a trip of a lifetime to Southern Africa. A coming of age, rite of passage, and what better place to explore this journey in. The Nature and the Human Soul describes a “center of gravity” shift as a person moves from childhood into adolescence. This father wanted a keepsake for his boys to cherise for years to come. I layered photos,drawings and captions of the animals so they will remember this adventure forever.
